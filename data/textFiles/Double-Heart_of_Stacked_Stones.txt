The Double-heart of Stacked Stones ( traditional Chinese : 七美雙心石滬 ; simplified Chinese : 七美双心石沪 ; pinyin : Qīměi Shuāng Xīn Shí Hù ) or the Twin-Heart Fish Trap is a stone fishing weir located on the north side of Cimei Township , Penghu County , Taiwan. It is a well-preserved ancient fish trap made by stacking stones to form a trap that resembles a flying heart.
