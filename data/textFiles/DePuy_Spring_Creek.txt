45°33′43″N 110°35′25″W ﻿ / ﻿ 45.56194°N 110.59028°W ﻿ / 45.56194; -110.59028 ﻿ ( DePuy Spring Creek ) DePuy Spring Creek is a three mile long trout fishery located between the Absaroka and Gallatin mountain ranges in Paradise Valley , south of Livingston, Montana . The creek is a small tributary of the Yellowstone River . This fishery supports a population of brown , Yellowstone cutthroat and rainbow trout .
