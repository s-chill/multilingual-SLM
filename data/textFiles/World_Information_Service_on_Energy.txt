The World Information Service on Energy ( WISE ) is an anti-nuclear group founded in 1978 to be an information and networking center for citizens and organizations concerned about nuclear power , radioactive waste , radiation and sustainable energy issues. From the very beginning they have published a magazine, meant to keep the global anti-nuclear movement updated on developments, arguments and campaigns.

Since 2001, the WISE has been affiliated with the Nuclear Information and Resource Service (NIRS).
