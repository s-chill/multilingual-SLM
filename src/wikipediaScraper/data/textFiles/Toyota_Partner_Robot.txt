The Toyota Partner Robots are a series of humanoid robots developed by Toyota . They debuted playing music on drums and trumpets at the 2005 World EXPO in Aichi, Japan .
There are 5 robots in all, most of which have different movement systems. The 5 robots are: Version 1 (bipedal robot), Version 2 ( Segway -like wheels), Version 3 (Segway-like wheels), Version 4 (unique wire system) and the i-Foot (mountable with 2 legs).
