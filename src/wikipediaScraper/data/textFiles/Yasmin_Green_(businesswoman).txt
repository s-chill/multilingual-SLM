Yasmin Green is the CEO of Jigsaw , a unit within Google that addresses threats to open societies. She leads an interdisciplinary team that researches and develops technical solutions to a range of global security challenges, including violent extremism, censorship, harassment, and misinformation . Jigsaw oversees both international cybersecurity and content moderation across Google's platforms. Green was the first employee at Jigsaw hired by founder Jared Cohen . Green and Jigsaw have developed a machine learning system known as Perspective which tracks online trolls by identifying their behavior.

Green is a fellow of the Aspen Institute and is a member of its Commission on Information Disorder. She is a member of the National Task Force On Election Crises, an initiative of the Protect Democracy project.
